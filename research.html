Research

Arab Protest Behavior in Israel’s 2023 Judicial Crisis

Master’s thesis combining survey data, fieldwork in Haifa, and interrupted time series analysis. Investigates intra-group variation in protest participation among arab citizens of Israel.

Religious Doctrine vs. Institutions in Political Speeches

Applies NLP techniques using fine-tuned sentence-BERT models to classify religious references in political speeches. Includes SHAP-based interpretability and time trend analysis.

State Reactions to Protest Movements

A comparative project using country-level and event-level data from Egypt (2011), Belarus (2020), and Russia (2012) to categorize regime types and analyze short-term response strategies.

Variation in Political Orientations in Israel

Clustering-based analysis of voter survey data to uncover subgroups, incorporating PCA and K-means clustering.
