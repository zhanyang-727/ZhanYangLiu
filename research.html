Research

Arab Protest Behavior in Israel's 2023 Judicial Crisis

Master's Thesis

Master's thesis combining survey data, fieldwork in Haifa, and interrupted time series analysis. Investigates intra-group variation in protest participation among Arab citizens of Israel during the judicial reform protests.

Survey Data Fieldwork Time Series Analysis Protest Behavior

Religious Doctrine vs. Institutions in Political Speeches

Ongoing

Applies natural language processing techniques using fine-tuned sentence-BERT models to classify religious references in political speeches. Includes SHAP-based interpretability analysis and temporal trend examination.

Natural Language Processing BERT Models SHAP Political Discourse

State Reactions to Protest Movements

Ongoing

A comparative project using country-level and event-level data from Egypt (2011), Belarus (2020), and Russia (2012) to categorize regime types and analyze short-term response strategies to mass mobilization.

Comparative Analysis Event Data Regime Analysis MENA Studies

Variation in Political Orientations in Israel

Ongoing

Clustering-based analysis of voter survey data to uncover distinct political subgroups within Israeli society, incorporating principal component analysis and K-means clustering to identify latent political orientations.
