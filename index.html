About Me

I hold dual Master’s degrees from the University of Michigan–Ann Arbor: a Master of International and Regional Studies and a Master of Science in Information (Big Data Analytics track).

My academic interests lie at the intersection of political science and data science. I focus on data mining, NLP, and data visualization applied to topics such as protest behavior, speech analysis, and regime response strategies in comparative politics.
